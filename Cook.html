Caramel Flan

I like making lots of desserts and cakes at home. One of my favorite things to make is Caramel Flan. For flan, I use 1 can of condensed milk, 6 eggs, 1 evaporated milk, and 1 cup of regular whole milk. I blend the ingredients together and set it aside to make the caramel sauce. The caramel sauce consists of 2 parts sugar and 1 teaspon of water. I cook it in a small saucepan and then transfer it to a oven safe tray. Once contents are in a oven safe tray I then transfer my blended flan mixture on top of the cooled caramel and place that tray in a bigger tray where I can steam the caramel flan with 1/2 cup of water in the oven. The caramel flan will need to bake in the oven for about 1.3 hours. Once it's done baking I place it in the freezer overnight and enjoy it the next day when it's cooled down to eat.

Brownies

My second top favorite dessert to make are brownies. I usually use boxed brownies mix but get a specific brand. I try to find the betty crocker brownie mix as its one of the most fudgey kind out there. I follow the recipe from behind the box and whisk dry and wet ingredients together, but ontop of that I also add a a cup of chocolate chips. That's my secret to getting really rich chocolatey brownies.

Mango Lassi

Third favorite item I like making is called Mango smoothie aka Mango Lassi. Mango Lassi is very easy to make you just need 4 ingredients. You will need 1 can of mango pulp or puree, 1/2 to 1 cup of sugar depending on personal preference, 2 cups of yogurt, ice, and 1 cup of whole milk. You blend all the ingredients together and pour all the contents in a glass cup with ice. Last, you can also add whipped cream on top of the lassi and a dash of sprinkles, but this step is optional.
